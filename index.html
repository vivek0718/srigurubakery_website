About Sri Guru Bangalore Bakery

Tradition meets taste in every bite

Our Heritage

Established with a passion for creating the finest baked goods, Sri Guru Bangalore Bakery has been serving the community with traditional recipes passed down through the past 10 years, combined with modern baking techniques.

Quality Promise

We use only the freshest ingredients and time-honored methods to create our delicious range of cakes, biscuits, breads, and specialty items. Every product is made with care and attention to detail.

Community Focus

As a local bakery, we take pride in being part of this Bakery community. We're committed to bringing joy to every celebration and making everyday moments a little sweeter.
